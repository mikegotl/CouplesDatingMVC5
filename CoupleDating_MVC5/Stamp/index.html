About

Our Short Story

Begun by a married couple for all couples. Frustrated with schedules of daily life and moving to a new city, it was hard to begin new friendships with people of similar interests in a new town. Hence, Couples Dating Scene was a solution to obstacles in finding new couples that we both felt comfortable with. Make this site experience your own! Relationship Rejuvenation

Don't miss it! Studies have shown, it will happen.

Remember...

...what it was like when you two began dating. You asked each other questions to get to know each other better and your answers surprised or even thrilled the both of you. These questions are usually the same when a couple makes new friends together and generates similar feelings that will have the both of you reminded of your beginning. That's always a good thing! Right?

Reinvigorate...

each other with the feeling of something new that you are both responsible for. This is a new relationship that you both create together. Studies have also shown that 'healthy competition' between two couples can improve one's self and the relationship as a whole. Don't miss out on learning how to do new things you have always wanted to do especially if new friends can show you the ropes. Such as paintball or skydiving! Ok, it can be anything you want and of course, how great would it feel to share something that makes you special as a couple with another couple.

Rejuvenate...
